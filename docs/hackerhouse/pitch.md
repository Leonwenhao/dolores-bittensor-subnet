# Hackerhouse Pitch

## One-Liner

Dolores is the task-supply subnet for RL agents: miners produce verifiable
software tasks, validators score them for frontier usefulness, and accepted
tasks flow into an open curriculum archive.

## Why Bittensor

Solver subnets need increasingly hard tasks. The open RL ecosystem is not only
short on solvers; it is short on verified, deduplicated, difficulty-labeled
tasks at the model frontier. Dolores turns that supply problem into an
incentive network.

## What The Demo Shows

Miner proposes a task package. Validator runs the Dolores verifier/scorer.
The task is accepted, rejected, or scored lower based on verifier quality,
novelty, and frontier value. Miner weights update from task quality, not spam
volume.

## What Makes It Different

- We reward producing the benchmark, not solving a fixed benchmark.
- We complement Ridges-style solver subnets.
- The output is useful outside crypto: a Hugging Face/GitHub task archive.
- The company can monetize off-chain hosted curriculum infrastructure.

## Company Split

- Dolores Research is the company.
- Dolores Autocurricula is the open engine and archive.
- The Bittensor subnet is the public proving ground and contributor funnel.
